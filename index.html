Portfolio

Doggy App:

Discover and adore your favorite dog breeds effortlessly! My app lets you select breeds from a dropdown menu, choose the number of images you want, and instantly fetches them from an extensive API. Explore the unique charm of various breeds through a visual gallery tailored to your preferences. Dive into the world of dogs with ease and delight!

Dodge the Enemies:

Enter 'Dodge the Enemies'! Navigate a red square through a deluge of falling green adversaries. Dodge, weave, and rack up points with every narrow escape. Challenge yourself to beat your own high score in this lightning-paced survival game. How long can you last?

Super Market:

Welcome to my innovative supermarket application, where convenience meets interactivity! My user-friendly interface allows seamless navigation through aisles by simply clicking on items of interest. Effortlessly add products to your cart, tailor your shopping experience, and proceed to a hassle-free checkout. Experience the future of grocery shopping at your fingertips with this interactive application.
